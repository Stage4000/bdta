About Brook's Dog Training Academy

Founded in 2018 by Brook Lefkowitz, Animal Behavior College Certified Dog Trainer.

Brook's Dog Training Academy is locally owned and operated in Highlands County, Florida. Our dedicated team is committed to guiding you on the journey to a stronger bond with your furry companion. We use positive reinforcement techniques and science-backed methods to help dogs and their owners build lasting relationships. Welcome to a place where training is not just a task but a shared experience of love and learning.

Certified Trainers

All our trainers are professionally certified and experienced.

Positive Methods

We use reward-based training for lasting results.

Personalized Programs

Tailored training plans for each dog's unique needs.

Proven Results

98% success rate with lasting behavioral changes.
